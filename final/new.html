about us

Located in San Diego, California, Blue Coaster Coffee puts quality first, ensuring each cup of coffee is freshly roasted, ground and served. With an in-house roaster located in San Diego, California, we seek to maintain the highest standard of specialty, organic coffee available on the market.

meet the team

Avery

Owner

Avery has been immersed in the coffee industry since the young age of thirteen. She has always been highly interested in in-house roasting and creating an inclusive cafe environment with unique menu additions.

Carter

Roaster

Carter has been Blue Coasters roaster for the last decade, working long and hard to achieve the utmost quality. Carter's passion is tasted in each cup Blue Coaster serves and is shown through Carter's frequent roast experimentations and additons.

Autumn

Lead Barrista

Autumn can make just about anything you throw at her. She has dedicated years to learning and immersing through the in's and out's of espresso pulling, proper tamping techniques, and even precise boil temperatures for tea's and pour-overs.
